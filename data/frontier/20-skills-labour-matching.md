# Skills & Labour Market Matching

**Frontier Service 20**

Training recommendations, job matching, and credential portability powered by data and AI.

## Exemplars

### Singapore

MySkillsFuture — a personalised skills and career guidance platform that recommends training programmes, tracks skills development, and connects citizens with employment opportunities aligned to their capabilities and market demand.

### India

PMKVY (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana) digital platform for skills certification and training matching. Connects millions of workers with vocational training and industry-recognised credentials.

### United Kingdom

AI Helpers pilot explicitly targeting employment transitions and apprenticeship matching as its first use case. Part of the broader GOV.UK AI programme to provide personalised guidance through major life events.

## Why This Matters

The future of work requires continuous reskilling. Digital platforms that match citizens with relevant training, verify credentials across borders, and connect trained workers with employers can dramatically improve labour market outcomes, especially for young people and those in transition.
