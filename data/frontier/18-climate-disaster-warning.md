# Climate & Disaster Early Warning

**Frontier Service 18**

Localised alerts, evacuation routing, and resilience planning tools for climate and natural disasters.

## Exemplars

### Bangladesh

Cyclone Preparedness Programme with digital alert layer. Bangladesh's system has saved millions of lives through increasingly digital early warning and evacuation coordination.

### India

IFLOWS-Mumbai (Integrated Flood Warning System for Mumbai) — a real-time flood prediction system that combines weather data, tide information, and rainfall measurements to provide advance flood warnings to city residents.

## Why This Matters

Climate change is making extreme weather events more frequent and severe. Digital early warning systems that reach citizens on their mobile devices can dramatically reduce casualties and economic losses, particularly in vulnerable countries.
