# Personalised Public Health Advisor

**Frontier Service 17**

Preventive, proactive health guidance tailored to individual risk profiles; AI-assisted diagnostics and triage.

## Exemplars

### United Kingdom (NHS)

AI Diagnostic Fund covering one-third of NHS chest X-rays (2.4M scans) with AI-assisted reads. AI scribes piloting across NHS trusts for clinical note-taking.

### Rwanda

Babylon Health partnership for AI-powered triage, providing remote symptom assessment and care navigation for citizens with limited access to healthcare facilities.

### India

Aarogya Setu for pandemic response and health advisory, reaching hundreds of millions of users during COVID-19 for contact tracing and health guidance.

## Why This Matters

Personalised public health moves healthcare from reactive to proactive, using data and AI to identify risk before illness occurs. This is especially transformative in countries with limited healthcare infrastructure.
