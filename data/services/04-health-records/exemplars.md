# Exemplar Deployments: Health Records

## Rwanda -- National Digital Health Platform
Rwanda operates one of Africa's most comprehensive digital health systems, combining OpenMRS for facility-level electronic medical records with DHIS2 for national health reporting. The RapidSMS system tracks maternal and child health, and the national health information exchange connects hospitals, health centers, and community health workers.

## Kenya -- DHIS2 and OpenMRS Integration
Kenya uses DHIS2 as its national health information system for aggregate reporting across all counties, while OpenMRS serves as the electronic medical record system in numerous HIV/AIDS treatment facilities. Kenya Health Information Exchange (KenHIE) connects these systems for longitudinal patient tracking.

## Estonia -- National EHR
Estonia's national electronic health record system, operational since 2008, provides a unified digital health record for every resident. All healthcare providers are legally required to submit data to the national EHR, and citizens can access their records through a patient portal. The system is built on the X-Road interoperability layer.
