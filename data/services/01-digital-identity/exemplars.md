# Exemplar Deployments: Digital Identity

## India -- Aadhaar
India's Aadhaar system, operated by UIDAI, has enrolled over 1.3 billion residents with a 12-digit unique identity number backed by biometric (fingerprint and iris) and demographic data. Aadhaar underpins authentication for banking, tax filing, welfare distribution, and mobile services. It is the world's largest biometric identity system.

## Estonia -- e-ID
Estonia's national electronic identity card provides every citizen and resident with a chip-based digital identity usable for signing documents, accessing e-government services, voting online, and authenticating with private-sector services. The system has been operational since 2002 and is a cornerstone of Estonia's digital-first governance model.

## Morocco -- MOSIP Deployment
Morocco adopted MOSIP as the basis for its national digital identity infrastructure, becoming one of the first countries to deploy the open-source platform at scale. The system supports biometric enrollment and credential issuance aligned with international standards.
