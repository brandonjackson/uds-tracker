# Exemplar Deployments: Population Registry

## Norway -- National Population Register (Folkeregisteret)
Norway's Folkeregisteret is a comprehensive digital population register maintained by the Tax Administration. It records all residents (citizens and registered immigrants), is continuously updated through civil registration events, and serves as the authoritative data source for all government agencies via standardized APIs.

## Israel -- Population and Immigration Authority Registry
Israel maintains a centralized digital population registry that records all citizens and permanent residents. The registry is updated in real time through civil registration events and is queried by virtually every government service for identity verification and eligibility determination.

## Rwanda -- National Population Registry
Rwanda has built a digital population registry linked to its civil registration system and national ID program. The registry supports social protection targeting, health service delivery, and electoral rolls, demonstrating how a low-income country can implement a functional population register using digital infrastructure.
