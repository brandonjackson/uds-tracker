# Exemplar Deployments: Public Procurement

## Ukraine -- Prozorro
Ukraine's Prozorro is one of the world's most successful e-procurement reforms. The platform, built on open-source software and OCDS, makes all public procurement data openly available. It handles the full procurement cycle from tender publication through bid submission to contract award, covering all government entities. Prozorro has been credited with saving billions in public funds through increased competition and transparency.

## Colombia -- SECOP II
Colombia's SECOP II e-procurement platform publishes structured procurement data using OCDS. It manages the full procurement process electronically and makes all contract data publicly accessible. Colombia was an early adopter of open contracting principles and has one of the most complete open procurement datasets globally.

## South Korea -- KONEPS
South Korea's Korea ON-line E-Procurement System (KONEPS) is one of the world's most comprehensive e-procurement platforms, processing the vast majority of public procurement transactions digitally. It integrates procurement with financial, tax, and business registration systems, and supports end-to-end electronic processing from supplier registration through payment.
