# Exemplar Deployments: Digital Payments

## India -- Unified Payments Interface (UPI)
India's UPI, managed by the National Payments Corporation of India, processes billions of transactions monthly. It enables real-time interbank transfers via mobile phone, supports QR-code merchant payments, and is the backbone for government benefit disbursements. UPI has become a global reference model for instant retail payment systems.

## Tanzania -- Mojaloop-based Interoperability
Tanzania deployed a national payment switch leveraging Mojaloop to enable interoperability between mobile money operators and banks. The system allows citizens to transfer money across providers seamlessly, significantly expanding financial inclusion in a country where mobile money accounts far outnumber bank accounts.

## Brazil -- Pix
Brazil's Pix, launched by the Central Bank in 2020, is an instant payment system that reached over 150 million users within three years. It operates 24/7 with zero or near-zero fees for individuals and supports P2P, P2G, and G2P transactions through a unified national platform.
