# 15 - Policing & Public Safety

## Tier
Essential

## What This Service Is
A government-operated digital system for citizen interaction with police and public safety services. This covers online crime reporting, requesting police reports, applying for background or clearance checks, reporting missing persons, and checking investigation status.

## What Counts as "Digitally Available"
- Citizens can report a crime or incident online through an official digital platform
- Police reports or crime reference numbers can be requested and received digitally
- Background checks or police clearance certificates can be applied for online
- Citizens can check the status of a reported crime or ongoing investigation digitally
- The system operates at the national level or covers the majority of police jurisdictions

## What Does NOT Count
- A website that provides emergency phone numbers and station addresses with no transactional capability
- An internal police records management system with no citizen-facing portal
- A crime statistics dashboard with no facility for citizens to report crimes or request services
- A tip-line or anonymous reporting hotline with no digital case tracking or service delivery

## DPG Maturity Level
**Gap** -- There are no widely adopted open-source digital public goods for citizen-facing policing and public safety services. Most countries use proprietary records management and computer-aided dispatch systems. Open data standards for crime reporting exist but full open-source citizen-facing platforms remain a gap.
