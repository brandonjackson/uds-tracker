# Exemplar Deployments: Policing & Public Safety

## United Kingdom -- Police.uk and Single Online Home
The UK operates Police.uk for crime mapping and local policing information, and the Single Online Home platform allows citizens to report crimes online, request police records, apply for firearms licences, and submit intelligence. Most of the 43 police forces in England and Wales provide online crime reporting through a standardised digital front end.

## Singapore -- Singapore Police Force Online Services
The Singapore Police Force provides comprehensive digital services including online police reports, e-services for permits and licences, application for certificates of clearance, and case status tracking. The Police@SG mobile app enables real-time crime reporting with location and photo attachments.

## Estonia -- e-Police
Estonia's digital policing services are integrated with the national X-Road infrastructure, enabling citizens to file police reports online, apply for background checks, and track case progress through the state portal. Police officers have real-time access to national databases during patrols via mobile devices.
