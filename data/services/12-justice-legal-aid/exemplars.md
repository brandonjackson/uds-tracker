# Exemplar Deployments: Justice and Legal Aid

## United Kingdom -- HM Courts & Tribunals Service Digital Reform
The UK's HMCTS reform program has digitized multiple court and tribunal services, including online civil money claims, online divorce applications, probate applications, and social security appeals. Citizens can file cases, track progress, and receive decisions digitally. The reform program represents one of the most comprehensive court digitization efforts globally.

## India -- e-Courts and Tele-Law
India's e-Courts project has digitized case management across district and subordinate courts nationwide, with the e-Courts Services portal and mobile app allowing citizens to check case status, view court orders, and receive hearing notifications. The Tele-Law program provides free legal consultations via video conference to citizens in rural areas through Common Service Centres.

## Singapore -- Community Justice and Tribunals System (CJTS)
Singapore's courts operate a fully digital case management and filing system. The Community Justice and Tribunals System allows online filing for small claims and employment disputes, with integrated online dispute resolution for lower-value cases. The judiciary also provides a digital court guide and self-help tools for litigants-in-person.
