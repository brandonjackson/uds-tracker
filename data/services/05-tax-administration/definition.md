# 05 - Tax Administration

## Tier
Foundation

## What This Service Is
A government-operated digital system for tax registration, filing, assessment, payment, and compliance management. This covers individual income tax, corporate tax, value-added tax (VAT), customs duties, and other fiscal obligations administered by the national revenue authority.

## What Counts as "Digitally Available"
- Taxpayers can register for a tax identification number online
- Tax returns (at minimum for one major tax type such as income tax or VAT) can be filed electronically
- Tax payments can be made through digital channels (online banking, mobile money, electronic payment)
- Taxpayers can view their account status, payment history, or compliance record online
- The system operates at the national level for at least one major tax type

## What Does NOT Count
- A website that provides downloadable PDF forms that must be printed, completed by hand, and submitted in person
- An internal-only revenue management system with no taxpayer-facing digital interface
- A taxpayer identification number system with no electronic filing or payment capability
- A pilot e-filing system available only to large corporate taxpayers with no access for individuals or SMEs

## DPG Maturity Level
**Early** -- There are no widely adopted open-source digital public goods for end-to-end tax administration. Most countries use proprietary or custom-built systems. Open standards for tax data exchange (e.g., OECD Common Reporting Standard) exist but full open-source tax platforms remain a gap.
