# Exemplar Deployments: Tax Administration

## Rwanda -- Electronic Billing Machines and e-Tax
Rwanda Revenue Authority operates a comprehensive digital tax system including online registration, e-filing, and e-payment for all major taxes. The Electronic Billing Machine (EBM) system requires businesses to issue VAT invoices through certified electronic devices connected to the tax authority in real time, dramatically improving VAT compliance.

## Kenya -- iTax
Kenya Revenue Authority's iTax platform provides online tax registration, filing, and payment for individuals and businesses. The system handles income tax, VAT, excise duty, and customs. Integration with mobile money (M-Pesa) for tax payments has expanded access to taxpayers without bank accounts.

## Estonia -- e-Tax
Estonia's e-Tax system, operational since 2000, pre-fills income tax returns using data from employers, banks, and other sources. Most citizens can complete their annual tax filing in under five minutes. The system is integrated with the national digital identity and X-Road infrastructure, enabling fully paperless tax administration.
