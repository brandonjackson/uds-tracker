# Exemplar Deployments: Immigration and Travel

## United Arab Emirates -- Smart Gates and GDRFA Digital Services
The UAE operates fully digital immigration services including online visa applications, smart gates with biometric verification at airports, and a digital residency system. The GDRFA (General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs) provides end-to-end digital processing for residence visas, work permits, and travel authorization through the ICA platform and mobile apps.

## Estonia -- e-Residency and Digital Visa
Estonia's e-Residency program pioneered a fully digital cross-border identity, allowing non-residents to establish and manage EU-based businesses remotely. The standard immigration process also supports online visa applications, digital residence permit applications, and electronic border management integrated with the EU Entry/Exit System.

## Australia -- Electronic Travel Authority and SmartGate
Australia was an early adopter of electronic travel authorization (ETA), allowing eligible travelers to obtain visa authorization entirely online. The SmartGate system at airports uses biometric (facial recognition) automated border processing. The Department of Home Affairs provides comprehensive digital services for visa applications, citizenship applications, and status tracking.
