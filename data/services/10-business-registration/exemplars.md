# Exemplar Deployments: Business Registration

## New Zealand -- Companies Office Online
New Zealand's Companies Office allows fully digital company registration, typically completed within minutes. The online registry is publicly searchable, supports annual filing, and integrates with the tax authority for automatic IRD number assignment. It consistently ranks among the world's easiest places to start a business.

## Rwanda -- Rwanda Development Board Online Registration
Rwanda implemented an online business registration portal that allows company incorporation in as little as six hours. The system integrates business registration, tax identification, and social security registration into a single digital workflow, contributing to Rwanda's rise in ease-of-doing-business rankings.

## El Salvador -- UNCTAD eRegistrations
El Salvador deployed UNCTAD's eRegistrations platform to create a digital one-stop shop for business formalities. The system streamlined multiple procedures (commercial registration, tax registration, social security enrollment) into a single online process, reducing registration time and cost for entrepreneurs.
