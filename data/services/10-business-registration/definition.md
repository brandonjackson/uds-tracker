# 10 - Business Registration

## Tier
Essential

## What This Service Is
A government-operated digital system for registering new businesses, issuing business licenses or permits, and maintaining a registry of legal entities. This covers company incorporation, sole proprietorship registration, trade licensing, and the maintenance of a publicly searchable business registry.

## What Counts as "Digitally Available"
- Entrepreneurs can register a new business entity (company, sole proprietorship, or cooperative) online
- The system issues a digital registration certificate or business identification number
- A publicly searchable digital business registry exists where company information can be verified
- At least the core registration process (name reservation, incorporation, certificate issuance) can be completed without a mandatory in-person visit
- The system operates at the national level

## What Does NOT Count
- A website that provides information about registration requirements and downloadable forms that must be submitted in person
- An internal database of registered businesses with no public search or online registration capability
- A business licensing system for a single sector (e.g., import/export licenses only) with no general business registration
- A planned online registration system that has not yet processed any applications

## DPG Maturity Level
**Emerging** -- UNCTAD's eRegistrations platform is the primary open-source solution, deployed in several developing countries. The space has limited open-source alternatives compared to other service areas.
