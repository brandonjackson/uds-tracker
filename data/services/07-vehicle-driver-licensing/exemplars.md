# Exemplar Deployments: Vehicle & Driver's Licensing

## United Kingdom -- DVLA Digital Services
The Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) in the UK offers comprehensive digital services including online driving licence application and renewal, vehicle tax payment, SORN (Statutory Off Road Notification), and vehicle keeper changes. The "View or Share your driving licence" service allows employers and insurers to verify licence details with consent. Over 90% of vehicle tax transactions are completed online.

## India -- Parivahan and Sarathi
India operates two major digital platforms for transport services: Parivahan for vehicle registration and Sarathi for driving licences. Citizens can apply for learner permits, schedule driving tests, renew licences, register vehicles, pay road tax, and transfer ownership online. The mParivahan mobile app provides a digital driving licence and vehicle registration certificate accepted by traffic authorities.

## Rwanda -- RURA Digital Transport Services
The Rwanda Utilities Regulatory Authority provides digital services for vehicle registration, driving licence applications, and transport licensing. The system integrates with national identity infrastructure for verification and supports online payment of fees.
