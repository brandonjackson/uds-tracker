# 07 - Vehicle & Driver's Licensing

## Tier
Essential

## What This Service Is
A government-operated digital system for managing driving licences, vehicle registration, and related road transport credentials. This covers licence applications and renewals, vehicle registration and transfer, road tax payment, penalty point tracking, and reporting vehicles as sold, scrapped, or stolen.

## What Counts as "Digitally Available"
- Citizens can apply for or renew a driving licence online
- Vehicle registration and transfer of ownership can be initiated or completed digitally
- Vehicle tax or road fund payments can be made through digital channels
- Drivers can check penalty points, licence status, or vehicle history online
- The system operates at the national level and covers the majority of licence and registration transactions

## What Does NOT Count
- A website that provides downloadable PDF application forms that must be printed, completed, and submitted in person
- An internal vehicle database used only by law enforcement with no citizen-facing portal
- A booking system for driving tests with no online licence application or renewal capability
- A regional or municipal-only system that does not cover the national jurisdiction

## DPG Maturity Level
**Gap** -- There are no widely adopted open-source digital public goods for end-to-end vehicle and driver licensing. Most countries use proprietary or custom-built systems from commercial vendors. Open standards for vehicle and licence data interchange exist in some regions but full open-source platforms remain a gap.
