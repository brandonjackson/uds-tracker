# Exemplar Deployments: Civil Registration

## Bangladesh -- OpenCRVS
Bangladesh deployed OpenCRVS to digitize birth and death registration across the country. The system integrates with health facilities for birth notifications and has significantly increased registration completeness, particularly in rural areas. It serves as the flagship deployment of the OpenCRVS platform.

## South Africa -- National Population Register
South Africa operates a comprehensive digital civil registration system linked to its National Population Register. Births, deaths, and marriages are registered electronically at Home Affairs offices and hospitals, with digital certificates issued and verifiable online.

## Estonia -- e-Population Register
Estonia's civil registration is fully digital and integrated into the national X-Road data exchange layer. All vital events are recorded electronically and instantly available to authorized government services, eliminating the need for citizens to carry or present paper certificates.
