# Exemplar Deployments: Social Protection

## India -- PM-KISAN and the JAM Trinity
India's social protection delivery leverages the JAM trinity (Jan Dhan bank accounts, Aadhaar identity, Mobile phones) to digitally disburse benefits for programs like PM-KISAN (farmer income support), MGNREGA (rural employment guarantee), and the Public Distribution System. Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) has reached hundreds of millions of beneficiaries, reducing leakage and duplication.

## Brazil -- Cadastro Unico and Bolsa Familia
Brazil's Cadastro Unico (Unified Registry) is a comprehensive social registry covering over 80 million low-income individuals. It underpins targeting for Bolsa Familia (now Auxilio Brasil) and dozens of other federal, state, and municipal programs. Digital registration, eligibility calculation, and payment through Caixa Economica Federal bank accounts form an integrated digital delivery chain.

## Togo -- Novissi
During the COVID-19 pandemic, Togo launched Novissi, a fully digital emergency cash transfer program that used mobile money and satellite data for targeting. Registration and payments were entirely digital, reaching informal-sector workers within days -- a notable example of rapid digital social protection deployment in a low-income country.
