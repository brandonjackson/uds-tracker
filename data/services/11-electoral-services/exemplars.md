# Exemplar Deployments: Electoral Services

## Brazil -- Superior Electoral Court (TSE) System
Brazil operates one of the world's most digitized electoral systems. The TSE manages a fully digital voter registry (biometric enrollment for over 120 million voters), electronic voting machines deployed nationwide since 2000, and real-time results transmission that typically delivers final nationwide results within hours of poll closure.

## India -- Election Commission Digital Infrastructure
India's Election Commission manages the world's largest voter registry (over 900 million voters) digitally. The Voter Helpline App allows citizens to check registration, find polling stations, and file complaints. Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) with Voter Verified Paper Audit Trail (VVPAT) are used across all constituencies, with results transmitted and published electronically.

## South Korea -- National Election Commission
South Korea's NEC provides comprehensive digital electoral services including online voter registration verification, real-time vote counting displays, and a candidate information platform. The system integrates with the national identity infrastructure and provides high-transparency, rapid results publication.
