# Exemplar Deployments: Agricultural Extension

## India -- Digital Green and eNAM
India's Digital Green program has reached millions of smallholder farmers through participatory video-based extension in multiple states. Separately, the electronic National Agriculture Market (eNAM) platform links agricultural markets digitally, providing price transparency and enabling online trading of commodities across state boundaries.

## Ethiopia -- Digital Green and 8028 Hotline
Ethiopia deployed Digital Green's video-based extension model across multiple regions, complemented by the 8028 agricultural hotline that provides IVR-based advisory services to farmers in local languages. The programs integrate with the government's agricultural transformation agenda and extension worker network.

## Kenya -- Kenya Agricultural and Livestock Research Organization (KALRO) Digital Extension
Kenya's KALRO operates digital platforms providing farmers with research-based agricultural advisories via mobile apps and SMS. The M-Shamba platform and similar tools provide crop-specific recommendations, market prices, and weather information, integrated with the government extension service network.
