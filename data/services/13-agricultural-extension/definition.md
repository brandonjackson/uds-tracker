# 13 - Agricultural Extension

## Tier
Essential

## What This Service Is
A government-operated or government-endorsed digital system for delivering agricultural advisory services, market information, weather alerts, and input supply chain support to farmers. This includes digital extension platforms, farmer registries, agricultural knowledge management systems, and market linkage tools.

## What Counts as "Digitally Available"
- Farmers can receive agricultural advisory information (crop management, pest control, best practices) through a digital channel (mobile app, USSD, SMS, interactive voice response, or web)
- The platform provides localized or personalized content (by region, crop, or season), not just generic information
- At least one interactive feature exists (e.g., two-way communication with extension agents, market price lookup, weather alerts)
- The service is operated, endorsed, or funded by the government agricultural ministry or agency
- The system reaches farmers at a meaningful scale beyond a single district pilot

## What Does NOT Count
- A ministry of agriculture website that publishes static reports and policy documents
- A private-sector agtech app with no government endorsement or integration
- A one-time SMS blast campaign during planting season with no ongoing service
- Radio or television agricultural programming without a digital interactive component

## DPG Maturity Level
**Emerging** -- Digital Green provides video-based extension tools deployed in several countries. DHIS2 has been adapted for agricultural data management. The space is growing but lacks a dominant open-source full-stack platform for agricultural extension.
