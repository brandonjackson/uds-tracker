# Exemplar Deployments: Education Platform

## India -- DIKSHA (Sunbird)
India's DIKSHA platform, built on the open-source Sunbird framework, serves as the national digital infrastructure for school education. It provides curriculum-aligned content in multiple languages, teacher training courses, and QR-code-linked textbook content. DIKSHA has been used by tens of millions of teachers and students across all Indian states.

## Saudi Arabia -- National e-Learning Center
Saudi Arabia operates a national e-learning ecosystem including the Madrasati platform for K-12 education, which became critical during COVID-19 school closures. The platform supports live classes, assignments, assessments, and parent engagement, serving millions of students across the kingdom.

## Uruguay -- Plan Ceibal
Uruguay's Plan Ceibal was one of the world's first national one-laptop-per-child programs and has evolved into a comprehensive digital education platform. It provides devices, connectivity, a learning management system, adaptive math tools, and coding education to every public school student in the country.
