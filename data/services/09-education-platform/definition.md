# 09 - Education Platform

## Tier
Essential

## What This Service Is
A government-operated or government-endorsed digital platform for delivering educational content, managing learner records, and supporting teaching and learning processes. This includes learning management systems (LMS), student information systems, digital content repositories, and online examination platforms.

## What Counts as "Digitally Available"
- A digital learning platform exists where students can access educational content (courses, lessons, assessments) online
- Student enrollment or learner records are managed through a digital system
- Teachers or instructors can deliver or supplement instruction through the platform
- The platform is endorsed, operated, or mandated by the national or subnational education authority
- The system serves at least one full education level (primary, secondary, or tertiary) at scale

## What Does NOT Count
- A ministry website that lists school addresses and policies with no learning or records management function
- A one-off distribution of tablets or devices with no accompanying platform or content management system
- Individual schools using private LMS tools (e.g., Google Classroom) without government integration or oversight
- A digital library of PDFs with no interactive learning, assessment, or student tracking features

## DPG Maturity Level
**Mature** -- Sunbird (India's DIKSHA backbone), OpenEdX, and Atingi provide robust open-source education platforms deployed at scale. Open educational resource standards (OER, SCORM, xAPI) are well established.
