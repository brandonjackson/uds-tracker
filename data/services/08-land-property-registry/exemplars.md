# Exemplar Deployments: Land and Property Registry

## Rwanda -- Land Tenure Regularization Program
Rwanda completed a nationwide systematic land registration program, digitizing records for over 11 million parcels. The Land Administration Information System (LAIS) provides a digital cadastre and title registry, enabling online land searches and digital transaction processing. It is one of the most comprehensive digital land registries in sub-Saharan Africa.

## India -- Digital India Land Records Modernization Programme (DILRMP)
India's DILRMP aims to digitize and modernize land records across all states. Several states (notably Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Karnataka) have implemented online land record systems where citizens can search records, request certified copies, and initiate mutations digitally. Integration of cadastral maps with textual records is underway.

## Georgia -- National Agency of Public Registry
Georgia implemented a fully digital property registration system that reduced the time to register property to one day. The system provides online access to property records, supports electronic submission of registration applications, and is consistently ranked among the world's easiest property registration systems by the World Bank.
